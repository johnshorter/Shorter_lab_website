Jonatan is a PhD student working on mathematical modeling of hypothyroidism. His research draws on large-scale Danish data sources — the Danish Blood Donor Study (DBDS) and registry data from Statistics Denmark — to characterize the disease and model its progression. He is currently preparing a paper titled "Pre-diagnostic Characterization of Hypothyroidism in the Danish Blood Donor Study."
