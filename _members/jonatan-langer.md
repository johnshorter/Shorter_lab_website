Jonatan is a PhD student working on mathematical modeling of hypothyroidism. He uses Danish data, including the Danish Blood Donor Study (DBDS) and registry data from Statistics Denmark, to characterize the disease and model how it progresses. He is currently preparing a paper titled "Pre-diagnostic Characterization of Hypothyroidism in the Danish Blood Donor Study."
