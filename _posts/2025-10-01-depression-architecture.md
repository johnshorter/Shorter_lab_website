title: "New paper: distinct genetic architectures for early- and late-onset depression"
author: John Shorter
tags: Genetics, Depression, Publication
---

Our genome-wide association analyses identifying distinct genetic architectures for early-onset and late-onset depression are out in *Nature Genetics*. The study points to partly different biology underlying depression depending on age of onset. [Read the paper](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-025-02396-8).
