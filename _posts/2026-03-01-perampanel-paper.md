author: John Shorter
tags: Pharmacogenomics, Publication

Congratulations to Sara Angelucci, who led our study on the pharmacokinetics of perampanel in Danish paediatric patients with epilepsy, based on therapeutic drug monitoring. The work is now published in *Basic & Clinical Pharmacology & Toxicology*. [Read the paper](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/bcpt.70209).
